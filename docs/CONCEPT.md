# Tell Me — Product Concept (General)

**Document type:** General / long-lived concept  
**Project type:** Solo hobby project (learning + growth-ready foundation)  
**Platform (initial):** iOS (SwiftUI, iOS 17+)  
**Language (v1):** English

---

## 1) One-liner
**Tell Me** is a couples conversation card game for intentional quality time: pick a pack, draw a question, and talk.

---

## 2) Why this product exists (Problem / Job-to-be-done)
Couples often want to have better conversations, reconnect, or explore topics deeper — but:
- it’s hard to start (awkwardness, no prompts, low energy)
- “we should talk” feels heavy
- planning a “date night” takes effort

**Tell Me** lowers friction to near zero: open the app → pick a pack → read a question → discuss.

---

## 3) Target audience (initial)
- Couples (20–40, broad)
- Use cases:
  - at-home date night
  - reconnecting after a busy period
  - deeper conversations without therapy framing
- English-speaking markets (initial)

---

## 4) Product principles (non-negotiable)
1) **Low friction**
   - Always “two taps to first question” as a guiding rule.
2) **Text-first clarity**
   - Readability and calm UI matter more than flashy visuals.
3) **Offline-first**
   - Works without internet; no backend required to play.
4) **Privacy-minimal**
   - No login/accounts by default.
5) **Taste & safety**
   - No explicit sexual content in early versions.
   - Avoid humiliating/toxic prompts.
6) **Pack-driven growth**
   - Content packs are the primary unit of value and future expansion.

---

## 5) Core experience (timeless definition)
A session is a simple loop:
- pick a pack
- reveal a card (question)
- discuss
- go next / skip
- end session anytime

The “game” is the conversation.

---

## 6) Content strategy (timeless)
The product is a **content + UX** business:
- Questions must feel curated, consistent in tone, and worth discussing.
- Packs must be distinct enough that users understand why a pack exists.
- The app should make it easy to keep playing (smooth navigation, minimal interruption).

---

## 7) Monetization philosophy (growth-ready)
- **Freemium** to allow immediate value and habit formation.
- **One-time purchases** for packs/bundles to monetize content in a simple, user-friendly way.
- Avoid subscriptions unless a proven content cadence and retention loop exist.

**Starter bundle model (initial):**
- Free preview experience with limits
- One paid bundle unlocking more content and/or removing friction
- Future growth via new packs and optional mechanics expansions

---

## 8) Differentiation hypotheses (not guarantees)
Tell Me can win (or be worth using) through:
- high-quality curation (less “random prompts”, more “good questions”)
- calm, premium-light UX that doesn’t feel like a cheap “question generator”
- pack structure that matches real relationship moments (reconnect, future, values, etc.)

---

## 9) Long-term expansion themes (directional)
These are “allowed directions” that fit the product identity:
- New packs (themes, seasons, collaborations)
- Packs with light mechanics (e.g., “write answers first”, “guess partner’s answer”)
- Optional “session formats” (15-minute guided flow)
- Localization (only after product-market feedback)
- Friends mode (later; separate experience from couples)

---

## 10) What this project is (and is not)
### Is
- A learning vehicle for shipping iOS apps properly
- A content-driven couples conversation tool
- A foundation that can scale via packs and feature modules

### Is not
- A social network
- A chat app
- A therapy product
- A backend-heavy SaaS

---

## 11) Success definition (for a solo owner)
- Ship v1 to the App Store
- Purchases + restore work reliably
- Users can get value within the first minute
- Reviews/feedback indicate “good questions” and “easy to use”
- A clear path exists to add new packs in future releases

---

## 12) Glossary
- **Pack:** A curated set of conversation cards (questions).
- **Session:** A continuous flow of card reveals while the couple plays.
- **Starter Pack / Starter Bundle:** A paid one-time purchase unlocking additional value/content.
- **Card Reveal:** The moment a question is shown to the user.
